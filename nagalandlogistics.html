By Train

To reach the northeastern state of Nagaland by Train, one can board any train that goes to the Dimapur Railway station. This railway station at Dimapur is the main railhead in the state and facilitates Nagaland Travel. Traveling to Nagaland by rail is very convenient for the tourists. The major railhead in the state is Dimapur. This Dimapur railway station is connected to Guwahati, which is the gateway to the northeastern part of India. The railway station of Guwahati is in turn linked to the other important parts of the country by several trains. All the important air lines in the country has a number of trains that connect the state of Nagaland to the different parts of the country.

Dimapur Airport

The Dimapur Airport is the main Airport of Nagaland. Located at a distance of 5 Kilometers from the center of the Dimapur City, the Dimapur Airport makes traveling to Nagaland by air easy. The Dimapur Airport is a public Airport and is operated by the Airport Authority of India. The city of Dimapur is well connected with all the other regions of Nagaland and therefore the Dimapur Airport is availed by many visitors to Nagaland. The Terminals of the Dimapur Airport are considerably large and has an arrival capacity of 250 and a departure capacity of 250. There are a number of major Airlines that have regular flights to and from Dimapur Airport and the most important among them are Indian Airlines and Air Deccan.

Nagaland Taxis

Nagaland Taxis are an essential part of traveling in Nagaland. Taxis in Nagaland play a major role in helping people to reach Nagaland by Road and to travel in the state Nagaland Taxis, also known as Nagaland Jeep and mini-coaches are always available from the taxi stands in the state. A large number of taxis link Dimapur and Kohima. One very easy and comfortable way of reaching Kohima from Dimapur is to take a rented car or a taxi. The Dimapur taxi stand is very close to the Railway Station. Yellow colored taxis are hired and one can either go for the shared taxi, which is to be shared with other passengers or the full taxi. Although the full taxi is a little high priced, the comfort factor that it provides should be kept in mind. Tourists can also go for a rented car from Guwahati, which will be obtainable from the travel agents. The drive is a very pleasant one and takes about five to six hours. It is suggested that tourists start the journey early in the day so that the destination is reached before night fall.
