Government Agencies


Here are some government agencies of Arunachal Pradesh:




1. Arunachal Pradesh Public Service Commission (APPSC) - The Arunachal Pradesh Public Service Commission is responsible for conducting recruitment examinations for various positions in the state government. It also advises the government on matters relating to personnel management and recruitment.

2. Directorate of Health Services, Arunachal Pradesh - This government agency is responsible for providing healthcare services to the people of Arunachal Pradesh. It manages and operates a network of hospitals, clinics, and health centers across the state.

3. Arunachal Pradesh Police - The Arunachal Pradesh Police is responsible for maintaining law and order in the state. It also plays a key role in combating terrorism and other forms of criminal activities.

4. Arunachal Pradesh State Transport Corporation (APSTC) - The APSTC is responsible for providing public transport services in the state. It operates a fleet of buses that connect different parts of the state.

5. Department of Education, Arunachal Pradesh - This government agency is responsible for the development and implementation of educational policies in the state. It manages the state's education system and oversees the functioning of schools, colleges, and universities.

6. Arunachal Pradesh State Electricity Regulatory Commission (APSERC) - The APSERC is responsible for regulating the electricity sector in the state. It sets tariffs and regulates the operations of power distribution companies.

7. Department of Agriculture, Arunachal Pradesh - This government agency is responsible for the development and promotion of agriculture in the state. It provides farmers with technical assistance, training, and financial support.

8. Arunachal Pradesh Forest Department - The Arunachal Pradesh Forest Department is responsible for the management and conservation of forests in the state. It is also responsible for implementing the state's forest policy and promoting sustainable forest management practices.
