Schools

Tripura schools are run by the state government or by private organisations, including religious institutions. Instruction is mainly in English or Bengali, though Kokborok and other tribal languages are also used.On 8 September 2013, the literacy rate of Tripura was declared to be 94.65%, which is the highest among all states of India. [1] Data from 2011 census of India reveals that overall literacy rate in the State was 87.75 percent with male literacy rate of 92.18 percent and female literacy rate of 83.14 percent.

Universities

Tripura University was formerly a wing of Calcutta University but eventually, it has become a Central University. The university is always known for its quality education & technological innovation in the field of academic & research achievements. The university has a total number of 46 departments offering 58 programs.
